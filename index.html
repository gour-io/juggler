Hello Everyone

We are Job Juggler, one of India's staffing & workforce management solution company.

We are head quartered out of Noida with presence in all the metro and other cities across the country. Job Juggler expertise in permanent hiring, staffing, and training and development.... Permanent staffing assignments are those when you look to hire people on your payroll on a parmanent basis.

Our Deliverable:

  • Sourcing of relevent candidates as per your mandate. (Sourcing through our own database / our partner network / placement agencies / Field recruitment teams)
  • Briefing the JD over the phone to shortlisted candidates / setting the expectations right and understanding their interest levels.
  • Scheduling interviews as per the timelines and venue details shared by you.
  • Irrelevent walk-ins / line ups won't be charged.
  • Better conversion rate as candidates will be lined up as per the checklist paramenters only.

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a wide range of staffing solutions to suit your needs including temporary, contract and permanent placements, seasonal employees, contract employees, bilingual recruitment, and executive searches. Whatever your staffing needs are, we’ll help you find the right solutions in a timely manner.

We do not charge from any candidates.

We serve a variety of sectors and industries across India including edtech sector, technical sector, warehousing and logistics, administrative and accounting, call center employment, finance and banking, and insurance. Our job is to provide your company with the most effective tools to hire candidates that are an excellent fit for your company, regardless of the industry you work in.

Our recruiters use a wealth of resources to help source national talent. We have access to various online databases that help us find creative ways of locating and attracting potential candidates to the positions our clients are looking to fill. We use social media platforms like Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, and Instagram to help generate awareness and interest in open positions. These mediums also help us to thoroughly research each potential candidate to get a sense of their likes, dislikes, personalities, and job-related experience. Additionally, we use state-of-the-art applicant tracking systems to keep an updated list of all potential candidates and make relevant notes on their profiles that’ll help you determine for yourself if they’re a suitable candidate for the position you’re offering. Throughout the entire process, we keep you in the loop and accept all feedback that helps us find the most plausible matches for your company.

Yes, we’re based in Noida, UP, India, but we’ve helped employers throughout the nation find suitable candidates for their businesses.
